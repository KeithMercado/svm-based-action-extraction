Threshold Tradeoff Interpretation
================================
Using high recall mode reduced missed actions (FN) by -10 but increased extra false alarms (FP) by -322.
Balanced mode: Precision=0.1692, Recall=1.0000, FN=0, FP=491.
High recall mode: Precision=0.3475, Recall=0.9000, FN=10, FP=169.
Thesis framing: If missing an action item is more costly than reviewing extra alarms, high recall mode is preferred for deployment.
