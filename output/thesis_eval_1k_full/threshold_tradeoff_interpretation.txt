Threshold Tradeoff Interpretation
================================
Using high recall mode reduced missed actions (FN) by 2 but increased extra false alarms (FP) by 2.
Balanced mode: Precision=0.5000, Recall=0.8000, FN=2, FP=8.
High recall mode: Precision=0.5000, Recall=1.0000, FN=0, FP=10.
Thesis framing: If missing an action item is more costly than reviewing extra alarms, high recall mode is preferred for deployment.
