Threshold Tradeoff Interpretation
================================
Using high recall mode reduced missed actions (FN) by 29 but increased extra false alarms (FP) by 326.
Balanced mode: Precision=0.7355, Recall=0.8444, FN=42, FP=82.
High recall mode: Precision=0.3865, Recall=0.9519, FN=13, FP=408.
Thesis framing: If missing an action item is more costly than reviewing extra alarms, high recall mode is preferred for deployment.
